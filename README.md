## Projects

### 1. AllLife Bank – Customer Segmentation Analysis
**Objective:**  
Segment credit card customers based on financial behavior and service interaction patterns to improve marketing effectiveness and customer retention.

**Key Highlights:**
- Applied K-Means, K-Medoids, and Gaussian Mixture Models
- Used PCA for dimensionality reduction and visualization
- Identified three distinct customer segments:
  - High-value customers
  - Stable mid-range users
  - Service-dependent customers
- Recommended targeted marketing, VIP programs, and digital adoption strategies

**Outcome:**  
K-Medoids provided the most balanced and business-relevant segmentation.

---

### 2. FoodHub – Food Delivery Optimization Analysis
**Objective:**  
Analyze food delivery operations to improve customer satisfaction, delivery efficiency, and revenue growth.

**Key Highlights:**
- Analyzed 1,800+ orders across cuisines, delivery times, and ratings
- Identified peak demand periods and high-revenue cuisines
- Found strong links between preparation time, delivery delays, and customer ratings
- Proposed operational improvements and targeted promotions

**Outcome:**  
Actionable strategies to reduce delays, optimize weekend operations, and promote high-value orders.

---

### 3. Marketing Campaign – Customer Segmentation and Targeting
**Objective:**  
Improve marketing ROI by segmenting customers based on demographics, spending behavior, and campaign engagement.

**Key Highlights:**
- Used K-Means, K-Medoids, DBSCAN, Hierarchical Clustering, and Gaussian Mixture Models
- Applied PCA and t-SNE for cluster validation
- Identified multiple customer personas:
  - High-income premium buyers
  - Price-sensitive shoppers
  - Campaign-responsive customers
- Designed personalized marketing strategies per segment

**Outcome:**  
Advanced clustering improved campaign precision and reduced wasted marketing spend.

---

### 4. SecondLife – Vintage Automobile Market Analysis
**Objective:**  
Segment vintage vehicles to support inventory optimization and targeted sales strategies.

**Key Highlights:**
- Analyzed vehicle attributes (MPG, horsepower, weight, cylinders, model year)
- Used PCA and t-SNE for dimensionality reduction and clustering
- Identified two dominant market segments:
  - Performance-oriented collectors
  - Economy-focused classic car buyers
- Linked technical vehicle features to buyer personas

**Outcome:**  
Delivered a data-driven framework for pricing and marketing vintage inventory.
